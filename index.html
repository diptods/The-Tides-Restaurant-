Our Story

Crafting Coastal Memories Since 1994

At The Tides, we believe that dining is more than just a meal—it's a symphony of flavor, atmosphere, and soul.

Born from a passion for local ingredients and the rhythmic beauty of the Atlantic, our kitchen focuses on "New American" techniques. Every plate is a canvas, and every ingredient is sourced from farmers and fishermen we know by name.

Our Philosophy

Nature's Rhythm,
Plated.

"The tide is the breath of the ocean, and the ocean is the soul of our kitchen."

At The Tides, we believe that fine dining should be an honest reflection of the environment. Our ingredients are sourced following the lunar cycles and seasonal shifts of the coastline.
